🔒 Privacy Policy — WA Direct Pro

Last updated: January 2025   100% Data Free

Summary

WA Direct Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Phone numbers you enter
  • ❌ Messages you write
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Personal information
  • ❌ Analytics or usage data
  • ❌ Any data sent to our servers (we have none)

Local Storage Only

WA Direct Pro uses Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API to save your preferences, history, favourites, and templates. This data:

  • ✅ Stays on your device only
  • ✅ Never leaves your browser
  • ✅ Can be cleared anytime from extension settings
  • ✅ Is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Permissions Used

  • storage — Save your history, favourites, templates locally
  • alarms — Trigger reminders you set
  • notifications — Show reminder notifications
  • contextMenus — Right-click menu on selected phone numbers
  • tabs — Open WhatsApp Web in a new tab
  • clipboardRead — Paste phone number from clipboard (only when you click Paste)

Third-Party Services

WA Direct Pro uses these free external services ONLY for QR code generation:

  • api.qrserver.com — Free QR code image API. Your phone number is sent to generate the QR image. No data is stored by this service.

No other external requests are made.

WhatsApp Disclaimer

WA Direct Pro is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with WhatsApp LLC or Meta Platforms, Inc. We simply open WhatsApp Web links in your browser.
